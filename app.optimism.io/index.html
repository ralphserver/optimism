Introducing the Optimism Collective

We're announcing a new type of community designed to reward public goods and build a sustainable future for Ethereum.

Public goods are the foundation of our society.

But they often go underfunded when incentives aren't properly aligned, forcing many to make trade-offs between earning a profit and building for the public good.

We’re seeing these same patterns play out in Web3. Optimism and Ethereum are public goods. The open internet is for all of us, and it will take all of us to help maintain it.

The Optimism Collective is a new model for properly rewarding those who create or sustain public goods.

The Optimism Collective is a band of companies, communities, and citizens bound by a mutually beneficial pact to uphold the fairness ratio: Impact = Profit

The Optimism protocol generates boat-loads of revenue, and the Collective allocates this money to projects and communities building for the common good.

The Collective consists of two houses: the Token House and the Citizens' House.

These houses are tasked with balancing long-term vision with short-term incentives.

By properly rewarding public goods, we can create a collaborative ecosystem that will grow and grow — creating a sustainable future for Optimism, Ethereum, and the open internet.

Governance will be iterative.

The systems that govern this collective depend on your participation as we build mechanisms to fairly fund this new world.

This is our initial commit. We look forward to your contributions.
